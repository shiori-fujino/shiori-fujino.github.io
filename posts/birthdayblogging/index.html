Birthday, Blogging

2021.06.01 onewheel

It has been a year after I refused to write. People still mention about my blog, even people who never knew about it started asking me, “I heard that you had a blog before…”

Blogging was fun, The funniest part was that it makes me feel like I’m worthwhile. It is fascinating that someone spends their own time to read my stories. Some of them even try to interact with me because they find me interesting from what I write. I mentioned too many times previously that I never got praised until I did blowjob and blog. lol Two big things in my life.

I bought Onewheel for my self birthday present. I’ve been riding a week straight and it’s lot of fun. I fell, rolled, flew, face-pancaked uncountable times. I got bruise all over my body and even on my face, now I’m a bit scared. Before, I was fearless because I didn’t know how painful it is until I really injure myself. With a little tiny bit of fear I ride more cautiously. It’s natural, it’s because I don’t want that pain again. Dating a guy is like that too. I met numerous guys without awe in last 10 years, heart was broken many times and I am not begging for relationship anymore, I’m preventing myself from any more injuries. Actually, everything is like that, You can do things without fear when you don’t know about things very well. It’s good that I am more cautious now but sometimes I miss fearless myself. I was more brave.

New night receptionist is same age as me and he has 3 jobs. I asked him “why you work so hard?” and he said “to save money and to buy a house”. It’s shocking, maybe because I never thought that way.
