Although, I thought it would be good to write again to rehab faster. People have to do something that they like to do. That’s what life is all about. Isn’t it?

If you read this now, then you must be one of my long-time follower, or… I don’t know how you get to here. This site is not SEO friendly, either reader-friendly website. I only write things to avoid “I-have-no-motivation-at-all” state. I think it’s from meds that I take. These meds are pretty shit; With a bit of exaggeration, I am human-vegetable these days.
