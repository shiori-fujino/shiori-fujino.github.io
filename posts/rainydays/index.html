I love rainy days! I think I am one week better and another week worse. I’ve been polar-bearing.

But I at least joined yoga again, as I mentioned in the previous post. I can’t get yoga-high, and I don’t expect dramatic weight-loss but trying to do something rather than hibernating.

Nurses also notify us of precious tea time,(Oh how can I not like thick, thick milo with milk) of course, they never forget to ask me if I’m doing great between any kind of activities, and finally, finishing with chitchat while getting goodnight pills. That’s it. Three nurses a day, many questions and much monitoring. Enough portion of attention.
