Why Took So Long

February 5, 2021

I had few goals to achieve before 2020 ends.

  1. Fix computers: I edited some system files (I think it was sudoer.d) from my desktop and laptop and now I can’t use it at all. I went to Apple store today, though they can’t help me either because I don’t have a proof of purchase(receipt)…
  2. Get motorbike checked up: Dashboard says to get serviced since late December.
  3. Make a new blog

I wanted to build a new blog since I closed my previous one. I was bored with WordPress. I wanted to try a new thing. I also found that there are many free options. (I was paying few hundred $$$ a year for WordPress business account. I spent money on no e-commerce, no other special features but merely writing weird, random shit.)

My first attempt for a new blog was Jekyll and failed because ruby on rails somehow didn’t work well with my macs. I had to do something about echo $PATH but never could work it out. Next, I had a look Gatsby, I have JS phobia so Next, I even tried to learn flask using python and gave up after lecture 5. database. SQLite hmm…? The most recent experiment was with Hugo(go lang), and I quite like it. Install, and configuration was smoother than others. This blog is made of hugo, and I’m not paying at all.

I spent days and nights to find a perfect tool(Jekyll, Gatsby, Flask, Hugo) which never seems to exist. I spent more days and nights trying to fix here a little bit, and then there, a tiny bit… and it never ends. By the time I’m already sick of what I’m doing. And then I look into other options. That is how I spent my six months of long-break from writing. But I’m giving up because I don’t think I can ever start writing if I keep searching for an ideal framework.

People say that it is nearly impossible to become a master of something without 10,000 hours of practice. A year is 8760 hours, and it’s my 10th anniversary. I have been working in the sex industry for ten years in 2021. A decade of dedication, devotion!!! This blog may not be the most aesthetical or high-tech portfolio filled with modern technology. But will be filled with something original and ingenious instead. From the latest specific market trend to an ethical contemplation about particular career…

Anyway, the moral of this story is: I still need to go procycles, st. peters and need to think about how I fix the apple disaster, but I finally made a new blog, and it took half a year at least. Big progress.
