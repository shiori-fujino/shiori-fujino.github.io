shiori-fujino.github.io

2021.06.03

Shiori-fujino.com was getting too hot. Gloriously, I got a psycho stalker too for the first time in my life. I wrote about private escort work with Celine when there was level 4 lockdown going on, also about crazy bitch ass stalker hayabi666. I also tried to write new things like interviewing Aoi.

In the meantime, my mental health was going downhill. I didn’t know that I was getting delusional. I was even locked in psychward for 15 days because of these unexplainable delusional thoughts. I wrote about the psych-ward experience after I got released.

Even under medication though, I couldn’t trust anyone especially if they are from the internet or my blog. I was very conscious about my blog. I chose to close my blog and deleted all data. My posts, my pics, and videos. That seemed to be helpful.

To delete the whole big ass data nourishing blog, I had to talk with WordPress. They asked me many times if I really want to delete it, they also suggested to back up in case I want to write again. I didn’t do anything because I was very delusional. I just hated everything, not only the blog but my life too. But the blog was my life anyway.

And next day, instant regret. lol

I had many hobbies, from Brazillian jiujitsu, yoga, cycling, to freediving, etc but none of those lasted more than a year or two. On the other hand, I wrote and maintain my blog for over 5 years.

But it is what it is, I dealt with it.

Time passed, covid gone, road got busier, back to work, everything back to normal. I stopped medication without discussing it with a doctor because I seemed very normal from my point of view. I started getting delusional again. Long story short, I got locked in psychward once again. lol

This time, they gave me a shot rather than pills to avoid situations like I stop taking medication freely without the doctor’s indication. And it was very very seriously horrendously mother fucking horrible. 😰

They had to stop delusional thoughts, so they stop me to think. I couldn’t feel/think anything under that one shot. It’s hard to explain but imagine you have nothing in your brain so you can’t feel/think anything.

“I’m not able to continue the conversation because I have nothing in my head.”

I used Hugo and GitHub, made shiori-fujino.github.io
